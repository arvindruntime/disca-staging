This a an open forum for general updates from the Care System.

General Update Board | 30 Jan, 2023 | By David John

Hi All,

This is a quick welcome to the 9 new managers who have registered with our Peer to Peer support group in Devon, Those of you who are new please do now login with the email address and password that you registered with.

This group is here to provider support to the care sector and we have training providers, HR advisers, insurance advisers in addition to each other to ensure the best advice can be found.

We have this portal that is driven by topics that you can start or follow and we have what's app groups should you wish to be added.

I look forward to work with you all.

John Tomes
